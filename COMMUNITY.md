## Enabling GitHub Discussions (for maintainers)

1. Go to https://github.com/credseal/sdk/settings
2. Scroll down to "Features"
3. Check "Discussions"
4. Click "Set up discussions"
5. Choose categories:
   - General
   - Ideas
   - Q&A
   - Show and tell

After enabling, the Discussions tab will appear on the repository.
