### FAQs

**Q: Do I need a commercial license for internal tools?**
A: If your internal tools are not distributed and you're using CredSeal Cloud (our hosted service), you do not need a commercial license. The AGPL only applies to distribution.

**Q: What about using CredSeal SDK in a SaaS product?**
A: If your SaaS uses CredSeal SDK and you don't want to open-source your code, you need a commercial license.

**Q: Can I try before buying?**
A: Yes. Use the AGPL version for evaluation. When ready to go to production with proprietary code, contact us.
